Introduction to Technology

The definition or general understanding of technology has changed from time to time. While very invention of science that is developed in order to make human work easy, efficient and quick can be regarded as technology. As the time progressed various technology were developed in order to facilitate human work in various field like information, communication, medicine, and so on. So, to define technology in one sentence itself is a massive task. While we talk about technology, most of the people refer it to as information technology and electronic devices because those are the form of technology which people use in daily basis. At this age of internet and social media the understanding of people I about technology has synchronized more into handheld devices and frequently used devices like smartphone, personal computers, internet and so on. While technology is a vast topic people often misunderstood technology with only the devices they use while categorize other for of technology like industrial technology as machinery and equipment. The general understanding of technology can differ from time to time as well. When we take a look on human evolution, knowingly or unknowingly human have been studying implementing basic principles of science in their life and been inventing various technologies well suited to their needs and what the time demands. For example: during stone age, invention of stone weapons and fire was the latest technology of that time. In the agricultural area various pulley systems were created which was the technology that time demanded. Coming to the present time technology has evolved around quiet a lot with inventions of artificial intelligence and has developed in an exponential way. But how much ever the technology has grown the basics behind every technology is science as it as in the past times. Discussing about the technology, social media, handheld devices and internet has been the most influential and have effected the daily life of each and every person around the world. So being the most influential form of technology, internet has various advantages along with its disadvantages.
