Negative impacts of technology

When we talk about the positive impact of the technology we cannot neglect the negative impact caused by it. Due to technological development in the various sector the world has been suffering with various consequences. Lets have a look on the negative impact of the technology:

  • Cyber crime:
    Along with the development of social media and internet, cyber crime has been increasing rapidly day by day. Cyber crime causes a mental health issue among people. Various problems like blackmailing, hacking, etc have been a major issue around the world. It causes a major hazard on peoples private and security.
  • Longer life vs better life:
    Despite the technological development of the health sector was unavoidable, the inventions of fertilizers and pesticides have cause a major health hazard. With the contaminated food we consumed our health takes a massive impact due to it. Even though we can have a longer life but with this we have to compromise on the quality of our life and health.
  • Problem of unemployment:
    The technological development and specially in the industrial sector, many human work force have been replaced with technology causing a major employment crisis. Now with the introduction of artificial intelligence this problem can furthur grow going into the future.
  • Environmental degradation:
    With the technological advancement the major impact have been upon the environment. Day by day the environment is degrading and the global warming is increasing rapidly. Due to this various consequences have been suffered starting from natural calamities. So the environmental issue should not be underestimated.

As we talk about this issue the list never seems to be finished. The development in technology has its own consequences. So, when we come to a common solution, the impact of technology whether it is positive or negative depends upon how we use it.
