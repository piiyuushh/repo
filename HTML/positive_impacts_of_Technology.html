Positive impacts of Technology

Technology now has been a integral part of every human being. Specially, internet social media, household devices, smartphones and computer has been the closest friend of human being. So we will also be taking it as the basis for analyzing the positive impact of technology. The positive impact of technology can be understood with the help of the following points discussed

  • Industrial development: The technology has been the base of industrial development. The first industrial revolution in 1760 in Great Britain happened due to the development of industrial technology where many machineries were used instead of human work force to produce various goods in the industry. Technology has also helped in research and development of new products.
  • Medical sector: With the development of medical technology the human life expectance itself has been improved and the average human now lives more due to available health services. Where people used to wait for death after they had caught diseases in the previous times, people now can cure even a life threatening diseases like cancer and heart attack only due to the technology.
  • Educational sector: Technology has been a key factor for the development of education sector. People living in one part of the world can easily know about the remains part of the world due to information technology and internet. Because of technology the research and development has been massively impacted. We knew about the importance if technology in the educational sector special at the time of COVID where even when the world was into lockdown the educational sector was somehow unimpacted due to online schooling.
  • Trade and business: Along with industrial development technology has positively impacted the trade and business sector. With the invention of e-commerce and digital marketing the trade now has been more easy and the businesses are growing massively with the proper use of technology.
    It is extremely difficult to put the positive impact of the technology into words as every sector has been impacted due to the development in the technology. Due to technology normal household work has been easier and more simpler. Internet and social media has created a state for people to express themselves. It has other positive impacts on sectors like security, communication, biotech, aviation, transportation, aerospace and so on.
